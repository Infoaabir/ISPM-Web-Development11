Sri Lanka Telecom PLC. (SLT) is one of Sri Lanka’s most valuable blue chip companies with a market capitalisation as at 31 December 2013 topping Rs.66 billion. Sri Lanka Telecom is the national ICT solutions provider and the leading broadband and backbone infrastructure services provider in the country. The two main shareholders of Sri Lanka Telecom as at year end were the Government of Sri Lanka which held 49.5% through the Secretary to the Treasury and Global Telecommunication Holdings N.V. of Netherlands, which owned a 44.98% stake. The balance shares are publicly traded.
